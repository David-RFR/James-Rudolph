About Me
Dedicated Family Law Attorney

James P. Rudolph is an attorney based out of Reel Fathers’ Rights, San Diego Office. With nearly 20 years of experience in law, his career spans working with top law firms, including Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd, where he focused on securities litigation and class action certifications. Mr. Rudolph also worked with WilmerHale in Washington, D.C., and Skadden Arps in New York. His resume includes time working with international leaders including Nigerian President Goodluck Jonathan and Muhammadu Buhari. He’s had the honor of meeting Prime Minister Tony Blair in London and German Chancellor Gerhard Schroder in Germany. In 2015, he served in the Bureau of African Affairs at the U.S. Department of State, focusing on international law and human rights.
